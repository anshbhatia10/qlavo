What is GEO? (Google-Validated Modern SEO)

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing your online presence so AI-powered search engines and assistants cite your brand in their responses. Google's official AI optimization guide (May 2026) validates GEO as the next evolution of SEO — stating that "optimizing for generative AI search is optimizing for the search experience, and thus still SEO." Unlike outdated SEO that only targets Google rankings, modern SEO optimizes for both Google Search AND AI-powered discovery through ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity AI, and Anthropic Claude.

As AI-driven search grows rapidly — 73% of Google searches now end without a click, and over 100 million people use ChatGPT weekly — GEO has become essential for businesses that want to stay visible in the AI-powered discovery landscape.

Based in Delhi, India, Qlavo serves clients across India, Dubai, and the United Kingdom, helping them build the entity authority and structured content that AI models look for when making recommendations.

Why GEO Matters in 2026

Traditional search is fragmenting. Users are increasingly asking ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini for recommendations instead of Googling. When they do, the brands that get cited are the ones with strong entity signals — consistent business information across platforms, structured data on their websites, content on high-authority publications, and mentions in trusted sources.

GEO is the practice of building these signals systematically. It doesn't replace SEO — it adds an essential layer for the AI-powered discovery era.
